## Project: Haile

The gait analysis tool is named **Haile** — after Haile Gebrselassie, the Ethiopian long-distance running legend.

**Haile** represents:
- Precision in movement analysis
- Athletic excellence
- Endurance and consistency
- The gold standard in running form
